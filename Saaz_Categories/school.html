PRINCIPAL KHADEEJA NAHEED

Khadeeja Naheed is a BS(CS) Scholar. I have been working as a Principal in the SAAZ School System for the last three years . I am honored and privileged to lead this esteemed institution, dedicated to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and character development. With 10 years of experience in education, I am committed to ensuring that every student receives a world-class education that prepares them for success in an ever-changing world.

Principal Message

SAAZ School System as an English Medium Institution, was established in 2023, to cater the needs of students up to matriculation examination. Our campus provide an atmosphere in which students can develop and grow as a model of excellence. School is a buzz with activities of a host of clubs and societies managed by students themselves under the guidance of their teachers, professionals and qualified instructors.

At SSS, we encourage the students to develop personal and social traits of their personalities, evolve their outlook towards life, mature their artistic and literary endowments. Here they listen to and care for the viewpoints of others, acquire self confidence and develop leadership qualities to shoulder the responsibilities of the future.
